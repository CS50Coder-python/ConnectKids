We connect children who lack access to extracurriculars with free online opportunities

We believe every child in the United States should explore arts, science, coding, sports and more. We partner with trusted providers and volunteers to match learners with activities that fit their age, interest, and schedule.

Mentors and volunteers

How it works

  1. Families or schools tell us age, interests and availability
  2. We match learners to vetted free online programs
  3. We support signup and provide low tech options when needed

For partners and volunteers

If you run a free program or want to volunteer please tell us about your offering. We handle outreach and scheduling so you can focus on teaching.

Accessibility and privacy

We design with accessibility in mind. We collect only the minimum information needed to match children with programs and we never sell personal data.
