The Opportunity Gap

Millions of children in the United States want to join extracurricular programs but cannot. Cost lack of transportation limited local options low bandwidth and minimal public awareness prevent children from exploring their interests and building essential skills.

22.6 million

Children whose parents want them in after school programs but cannot access one.

77%

Of interested families are turned away because programs are full too expensive or unavailable in their area.

43%

Of U.S. children participate in no extracurricular activities at all indicating huge access gaps.

A problem with little attention

Studies show extracurricular access is one of the strongest predictors of later academic success yet national funding and public awareness remain extremely low. Compared to food security technology access or standardized testing extracurricular inequality receives far less national focus despite being deeply linked to emotional development creativity and opportunity.

"Most families who want after school learning support cannot find it. The opportunity gap grows each year while demand keeps rising."

Why ConnectKids exists

Despite huge unmet demand free and online extracurriculars exist but are scattered and unreachable to many families. ConnectKids bridges that gap by collecting trusted free programs and making them easy to access regardless of location or income.
