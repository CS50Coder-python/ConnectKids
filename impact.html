Projected Impact Scale

This scale shows how ConnectKids can move the needle over time by increasing access, participation, and long-term outcomes.

Level 1 — Awareness & Matching

Collect and surface free programs. Target: reach 1 million families within 12 months.

+1M families

Level 2 — Participation Boost

Reduce the number of children missing extracurriculars by improving signups and providing low-tech options. Target: convert 10% of currently unmet demand within 2 years.

~2.26M children

Level 3 — Skill & Opportunity Growth

Sustained participation leads to measurable gains in soft skills, STEM interest, and college/career readiness over 5 years.

Improved outcomes

Level 4 — Systems Change

Partner with schools and funders to embed free extracurricular access into public education supports.

Policy adoption

These scales are conservative projections to show achievable milestones. Exact numbers will depend on funding, partnerships, and volunteer capacity.
